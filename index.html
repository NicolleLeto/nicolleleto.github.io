About

I was born in a small Brazilian city, in the state of Bahia, called Poções. I moved to another city in the same state, Dias D’àvila, when I was a baby. I was a very curious child, I used to say that I would be the "forest girl". And I was a forest girl, a dancer, an astronaut, a scientist, an actress, a singer and a lot of things that a child that a child would like to be.

Nowadays, I am 18 years old, a young woman totally in love with science and the positive changes that it can cause in humanity. I think science is beautiful, it gives me a special glow in the look. I am a dreamer and my dreams include a better world for us, I know it sounds cliché or Miss Universe speech thing, but what is life other than trying to do the best?

I like to work with quality education, encouraging other young people to love sciences and change their lives with real opportunities. I also like to work with informatics applied to the health area. I am interested in the areas of biosciences, bioinformatics, chemistry and human health and well-being. I am also interested in nerd culture, I can’t even wait to the new Guardians of the Galaxy. My biggest goal is to become a scientist with social responsibility. Being able to change the reality for better.
